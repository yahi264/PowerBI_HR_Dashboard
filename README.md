# HR Analytics & Employee Performance Dashboard

## Introduction
This project is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce dynamics for a company of 1,470 employees. It helps HR managers quickly identify employees due for promotion and those at risk of retrenchment (layoffs).

The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable lists, making it easier to make data-driven decisions about talent retention and career progression.

## Features & Insights

* **Employee Demographics:** Analyzes gender distribution (60% Male, 40% Female) and distance from work.
* **Promotion Tracking:** Automatically identifies the **72 employees** (4.9%) who are due for a promotion based on their service years and job level.
* **Retrenchment Risk:** Highlights **117 employees** (8%) who are at risk of being retrenched, helping HR take preventative action.
* **Navigation:** Uses a simple sidebar with buttons to switch between the Home, Action, and Detail pages.
* **Department Analysis:** Breaks down data by R&D, Sales, and HR to see which departments have the highest risks.
